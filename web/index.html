Transparency: historically the model is break-even against the market — we don't hide this number. Decision-support analysis, not a betting operator.
These are the model's own probabilities and the fair odds derived from them — with no bookmaker margin, so they are not real market odds. How to use them: check the real odds at your bookmaker, and if they are higher than the fair odds shown here, the model considers it potential value; if lower, avoid it. Where we can fetch real market odds (in-season leagues), the value is flagged automatically (model vs market); elsewhere, compare manually.
